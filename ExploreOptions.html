Clinical Resources

Materials and tools that support healthcare providers in delivering safe and effective care.

  • Up-to-date clinical guidelines and protocols.

Hospital Operations

Hospitals operate as integrated systems where every department and staff member contributes to delivering effective, safe, and timely healthcare services

  • Stepwise guidance for patient movement between wards or departments.
  • Interactive schedules to view shifts and manage workload.
  • Tools to track medical supplies, repairs, or new equipment.
  • Supports efficient workflow and smooth hospital logistics.

Quality and Safety

Hospital staff work together to ensure patient safety, maintain high-quality care, and continuously improve hospital practices.

  • Tools to track compliance, review incident logs, and generate statistics.
  • Procedures for reporting incidents and handling emergencies.
  • Monitoring Ejada performance and staff engagement.
  • Enhances patient safety and ensures hospital-wide quality standards are maintained.

Nursing HR

Nursing HR uses a dedicated dashboard to manage staffing, track performance, coordinate training, and support the professional development of the nursing team.

  • Quick access to names, roles, and contacts of PHDU staff.
  • View completed courses, expiration dates, and certifications.
  • Tools to evaluate staff skills and development.
  • Attendance tracking, HR forms, and other documentation.
